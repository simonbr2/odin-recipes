Bacon Cheese Burger

Below you'll find the steps to create a quick, easy, and delicious burger

First, here are a few things you'll need:

  • (1 lb) ground beef or large portabella mushroom for vegetarian
  • (1 tsp) ground black pepper
  • (6 slices) cheddar cheese
  • (6 slices) fresh cooked bacon
  • (6) kaiser rolls
  • (6-8 dashes) tabasco sauce

To prepare the meal, simply follow these steps:

  1. Place beef into a mixing bowl with tabasco sauce and pepper and mix thoroughly
  2. Slice the rolls in half and place the rolls face down on a skillet over medium heat
  3. Heat the skillet to medium high heat. Form the beef mixture into patties and place in the skillet.
  4. Allow each side to cook for roughly 4 minutes. After the first flip, place a slice of cheddar cheese on to melt
  5. When the patties are done, place them on the halved buns and top each with 2 pieces of bacon
  6. EAT
