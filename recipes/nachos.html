Oven Baked Nachos

It's hard to beat a warm plate of homemade oven baked nachos on a cold winter day. Below I'll walk you through the steps to creating your own delicious meal!

First, here are a few things you'll need:

  • (1) bag of tortilla chips
  • (1) whole lime
  • (2) fresh jalapenos
  • (1) fresh avocado
  • (1) can of corn
  • (1) bag of shredded cheese or block of cheese
  • (1) red pepper
  • (1/2 lb) ground beef or jack fruit

To prepare the meal, simply follow these steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 deg F
  2. Brown the meat or jack fruit
  3. cut fresh avocado and remove pit
  4. dice jalapenos and red pepper
  5. place one layer of chips on a baking dish
  6. evenly distribute the meat (or jackfruit), corn, and diced peppers
  7. cover the chips with cheese!
  8. bake in the oven for approximately 15 min (keep an eye on it and pull it out when the cheese looks melted)
  9. spread guacamole on cheaps or leave it to the side for dipping
  10. spread sour cream and / or salsa across the chips
  11. EAT
